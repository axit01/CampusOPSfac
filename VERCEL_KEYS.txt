=== AAVISHKAR FACULTY PORTAL - VERCEL KEYS ===

To make the faculty portal work on Vercel, you must add these
environment variables in the Vercel Dashboard (Settings > Environment Variables).

1. VITE_API_URL
   Value: [Your Live Backend URL]/api
   (Example: https://aavishkar-admin.vercel.app/api)

2. VITE_SOCKET_URL
   Value: [Your Live Backend URL]
   (Example: https://aavishkar-admin.vercel.app)

Note: Ensure your backend (Admin side) is deployed before 
setting these keys.
